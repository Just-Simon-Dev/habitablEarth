Climate vs. Weather

Sometimes this two words we are using as the same thing. It's very similar, but have some differences. The climate contains some "variables" and one of them is weather. When we are saying the climate is warmer today, it's not means it's hotter in the world. To visualize the difference, we can imagine a dog (weather) and the human with leash (climate). If we will have a smaller leash the dog can go to the left and to the right in smaller range, but if we will have a longer leash the dog will have longer range to go. The same thing is in climate when it's change, the weather will be more extremal than today, we will have very freezing winter and very hot summer. Also we can observate very strong storms than in the past.
